Before we went to Canada we saw the Niagara Falls from the US side. Then we drove to the Canada side and we went on a boat near the falls. After that we went behind the falls. Which means we literally went behind the falls. If you stick your hand out you can touch the water. After we went to the falls we went back to our hotel. The next day we went to the CN towers. We went up to the top and ate dinner there. We got tiny coffee stirrers that were in the shape of the CN tower. That was my very fun trip to Canada.
