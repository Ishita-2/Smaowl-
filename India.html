I went to India a bunch of times so I am going to write about the most resent time I went. I went to India for my cousin's wedding. I was the flower girl, but in an Indian wedding when you are the flower girl it means that you stay by who ever is getting married the whole wedding. So my cousin Sneha and I stayed together most of the wedding. It was very fun!! :) We stayed in India for two weeks, we were hedding toward the airport and we stayed there of 24 hours waiting for our plane but it was delayed, for a week!!! In that week we went to a cooking class with our friends. We made ice-cream, garlic bread, fruit salad, pudding, cupcakes, milkshakes, and smoothies. That was my India trip!
